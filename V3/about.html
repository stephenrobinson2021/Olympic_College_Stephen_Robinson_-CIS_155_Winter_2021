About me:

I moved to the PACNORWEST in 1984. I became interested in hiking and camping at an early age. Over the last 37 years I've hiked and camped on over 200 miles of trails in Washington State. There's hundereds of miles of wilderness to explore. Even when I've hiked the same trail more than once there is always something new to see.
